Have your earthcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. earthcoin Wallet is the first mobile earthcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of earthcoin amount in DBG and mDBG.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of earthcoin via NFC, QR-codes or earthcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used earthcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for earthcoin balance.

You can find more about earthcoin at
http://earthcoin.co

If you want to contribute to earthcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/earthcoin/android/earthcoin-wallet
